About JKPG

Jönköping is the 10th biggest city in Sweden, and is located at the bottom of the second biggest lake in Sweden, called Vättern. JKPG as Jönköping is called is a city were everything you need is close, it is both small and big. Here in JKPG you can take a walk with beautiful surroundings, "fika", eat lunch at "bryggan" or go out and drink/dance at diffrent clubs.
