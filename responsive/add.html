Figma, a streamingsite

We got an assignment to create a streaming site, and I decided to do one about feelgood movies. We created these ones in Figma and it was really fun and easy. I decided to go with a blue background one the clickable sites and a picture of a sky on the front site. Simple and happy was the vibe that I were searching for. I created a site for feelgood which is movies which is funny and not at all draining for your mind. Neither are they scary or unpleasent. This is also something that I think is missing in exicting streamingsites.
